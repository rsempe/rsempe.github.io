Studios in Provence (2 people)

Independent Studios Bed & Breakfast in Carpentras

Welcome to Les Célestins, a renovated Provençal farmhouse in the heart of a family market garden. Two independent studios welcome you.

5 min by car from the centre of Carpentras (20 min walk) and 7 km from Wave Island and Spirou parks.

Provençal market, Vaucluse villages, Mont Ventoux... or relaxation by the pool.

Studio The Cabanon

25 m² studio with equipped kitchenette, double bed (160 cm), private shower room and toilet.

Air conditioning, shaded terrace with pool view and barbecue available.

Breakfast, linen and towels included.

  • Individual air conditioning
  • Large double bed 160 cm
  • Independent bathroom and toilet
  • Wardrobe and hanging space
  • Free WiFi
  • Included Breakfast

Studio The Roseraie

25 m² studio with equipped kitchenette, double bed (160 cm), private shower room and toilet.

Air conditioning, terrace with garden view and barbecue available.

Breakfast, linen and towels included.

  • Individual air conditioning
  • Large double bed 160 cm
  • Independent bathroom and toilet
