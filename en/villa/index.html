Villa in Provence (6 people)

The Villa Cottage in Carpentras for 6 people

Peaceful villa, fully fenced with complete privacy, ideal for a relaxing holiday.
Private pool and air conditioning in every room.

Just 5 minutes by car from the centre of Carpentras and 10 km from Wave Island and Spirou parks.

Enjoy the Provencal market, explore the villages of Vaucluse (Gordes, Roussillon, L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue...), climb Mont Ventoux or relax by the pool.

Entertainment area
Swimming Pool
Boules Court
Patio

This 105 m² villa offers a bright living space with lounge, dining area and open kitchen, as well as 3 bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom with WC. The entire property is fully air-conditioned for your comfort.

Outside, enjoy an enclosed 800 m² garden, a boules court and a private pool.

Barbecue, ping pong table and foosball complete the amenities for fun moments with family or friends.
